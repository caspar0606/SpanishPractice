Three directions for making the app feel more intentional.

These are lightweight static mockups using your current brand and layout as a base. One leans premium and calm, one leans playful and motivating, and one pushes much further into a game-like reward loop.

Option A

Modern SaaS

Cleaner typography, softer glassmorphism, stronger spacing, and a more premium editorial feel.

  • Subtle gradients and elevated cards
  • Premium serif heading + quiet status surfaces
  • Feels polished without becoming distracting
Open modern mockup

Option B

Playful / Gamey

Brighter surfaces, badges, more expressive accents, and slightly gamified momentum cues.

  • Colorful reward cues and chips
  • Friendly “XP / streak” energy
  • Still readable, but more personality-forward
Open playful mockup

Option C

Arcade / High-energy

Darker neon surfaces, stronger motion cues, and louder progression feedback without leaning fully into game copy.

  • Most “sticky” and personality-led
  • Feels like a lively, high-energy dashboard
  • Best if motivation loops matter more than restraint
Open arcade mockup

Suggested next step: pick one direction, then port its strongest ideas into `frontend/static/styles.css`.
